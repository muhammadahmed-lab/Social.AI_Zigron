## TASK
Generate high-conversion messaging for 4 platforms following the StoryBrand 7-Part Framework (SB7):

### 1. LinkedIn (Professional Narrative)
- **Hook**: Pivot on the Philosophical problem (Why is this wrong/unfair?).
- **Body**: Show empathy for the internal frustration. Present a 3-step Plan.
- **Closing**: Call to Action (CTA) that leads to a "Character Transformation."

### 2. Instagram (Visual Storytelling)
- **Hook**: Relatable "Before" state (the Internal problem).
- **Body**: Micro-plan showing the Guide’s assistance. 
- **Closing**: Direct CTA for a quick win.

### 3. Twitter/X (Concise Authority)
- **Format**: Villain + Plan + Victory.
- **Content**: A single punchy insight that resolves the External problem.

### 4. YouTube (Engagement Script)
- **0-10s**: The Hook (State the Villain/Problem clearly).
- **Body**: Define the Empathy (I understand) + Authority (I have the solution). Lay out the Plan.
- **Outro**: Clear direct CTA to avoid failure.
